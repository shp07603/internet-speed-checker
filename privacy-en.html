Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how My Network Score (hereinafter 'the Service') collects, uses, and protects users' personal information.

1. Items of Personal Information Collected

This Service can be used without separate membership registration, and the following information may be automatically generated and collected during the service use process.

  • User's IP address, cookies, visit date and time, service use record, bad use record
  • Device information (operating system, browser type and version, screen resolution, etc.)
  • Internet speed measurement result data (download/upload speed, ping, jitter, etc.)

2. Purpose of Collection and Use of Personal Information

The collected information is used only for the following purposes.

  • Service provision and quality improvement (improving speed measurement accuracy, etc.)
  • New service development and utilization for marketing and advertising
  • Understanding connection frequency and statistics on service use

3. Operation and Refusal of Cookies

This Service uses 'cookies' to store and retrieve usage information from time to time in order to provide individual customized services to users.

Users have the right to choose whether to install cookies. By setting options in the web browser, you can allow all cookies, go through a confirmation every time a cookie is saved, or refuse to save all cookies.

Google AdSense Cookies: This site may display advertisements through Google AdSense. Google uses cookies to provide customized advertisements based on the user's visit record to the site.

4. Provision of Personal Information to Third Parties

In principle, this Service does not provide users' personal information to external parties. However, exceptions are made in cases where there is a request from an investigative agency in accordance with the procedures and methods prescribed by laws and regulations for the purpose of investigation.

5. Personal Information Protection Officer

For inquiries regarding personal information protection related to service use, please contact the contact information at the bottom of the website.
